Biryani Recipe

Description

Chicken biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of spices, often including saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon. It is typically cooked using the dum (steam) method, which allows the flavors to meld together, resulting in a rich and satisfying meal enjoyed in many cultures, especially in South Asia
